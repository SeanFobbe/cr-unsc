173 (1962). Resolution of 26 July 1962
[S/5150]

The Security Council,

Having examined the application of the Kingdom of
Burundi for admission to the United Nations, 13

Recommends to the General Assembly that the Kingdom of Burundi be admitted to membership in the
United Nations.

Adopted unanimously at the
1017th meeting.

13 Ibid., documents S/5139 and Add.1.
