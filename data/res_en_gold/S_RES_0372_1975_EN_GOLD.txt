Resolution 372 (1975)
of 18 August 1975

The Security Council,

Having examined the application of the Republic of
Cape Verde 45 for admission to the United Nations,

Recommends to the General Assembly that the Republic of Cape Verde be admitted to membership in
the United Nations.

Adopted unanimously at the
838th meeting.


45 Official Records of the Security Council, Thirtieth Year,
Supplement for July, August and September 1975, document
S/11800.
