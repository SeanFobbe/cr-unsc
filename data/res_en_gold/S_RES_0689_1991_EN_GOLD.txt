Resolution 689 (1991)
of 9 April 1991

The Security Council,
Recalling its resolution 687 (1991) of 3 April 1991,

Acting under Chapter VII of the Charter of the United
Nations,

1. Approves the report of the Secretary-General of 5 and
9 April 1991 on the implementation of paragraph 5 of Security
Council resolution 687 (1991); 66

2. Notes that the decision to set up an observer unit was
taken in paragraph 5 of resolution 687 (1991) and that the unit
can be terminated only by a further decision of the Council; the
Council shall therefore review the question of its termination
or continuation every six months;
3. Decides that the modalities for the initial six-month
period of the United Nations Iraq-Kuwait Observation Mission
shall be in accordance with the above-mentioned report and
shall also be reviewed every six months.

Adopted unanimously at the 2983rd
meeting.
