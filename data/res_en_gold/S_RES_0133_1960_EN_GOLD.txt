133 (1960). Resolution of 26 January 1960
[S/4258 and Add.1]

The Security Council,

Having examined the application of the Republic of
Cameroun, 16

Recommends to the General Assembly that the Republic of Cameroun be admitted to membership in the
United Nations.

Adopted unanimously at the
850th meeting.

16 Official Records of the Security Council, Fifteenth Year, Supplement for January, February and March 1960, document S/4256.
