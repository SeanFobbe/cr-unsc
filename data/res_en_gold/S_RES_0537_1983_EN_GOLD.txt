Resolution 537 (1983)
of 22 September 1983

The Security Council,

Having examined the application of Saint Christopher and Nevis for admission to the United
Nations, 79

Recommends to the General Assembly that Saint
Christopher and Nevis should be admitted to membership in the United Nations.

Adopted unanimously at the
2479th meeting.

79 Official Records of the General Assembly, Thirty-eighth Session, Annexe.s, agenda item 19, document A/38/424-S/15989.
