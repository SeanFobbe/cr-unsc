175 (1962). Resolution of 12 September 1962
[8/5167]

The Security Council,

Having examined the application of the State of
Trinidad and Tobago for admission to the United
Nations, 15

Recommends to the General Assembly that the State
of Trinidad and Tobago be admitted to membership in
the United Nations.

Adopted unanimously at the
1018th meeting.

15 Ibid., documents S/5162 and Add.1.
