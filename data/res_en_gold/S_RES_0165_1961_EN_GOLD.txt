165 (1961). Resolution of 26 September 1961
[S/4955]

The Security Council,

Having examined the application of Sierra Leone for
admission to the United Nations, 11

Recommends to the General Assembly that Sierra
Leone be admitted to membership in the United Nations.

Adopted unanimously at the
968th meeting.

11 Ibid., Sixteenth Year, Supplement for April, May and June
1961, document S/4797.
