Resolution 223 (1966)
of 21 June 1966

The Security Council,

Having examined the application of Guyana for
admission to the United Nations, 23

Recommends to the General Assembly that Guyana
be admitted to membership in the United Nations.

Adopted unanimously at the
1287th meeting.


23 Official Records of the Security Council, Twenty-first Year,
Supplement for April, May and June 1966, document S/7349.
