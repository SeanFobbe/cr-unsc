112 (1956). Resolution of 6 February 1956
[S/3546]

The Security Council,
Having examined the application of the Sudan, 14

Recommends to the General Assembly that the Sudan
be admitted to membership in the United Nations.

Adopted unanimously at the
716th meeting.

14 Official Records of the Security Council, Eleventh Year, Supplement for January, February and March 1956, document S/3543.
