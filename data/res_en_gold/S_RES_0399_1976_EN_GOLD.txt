Resolution 399 (1976)
of 1 December 1976

The Security Council,

Having examined the application of Western Samoa 85 for
admission to the United Nations,

Recommends to the General Assembly that Western
Samoa be admitted to membership in the United Nations.

Adopted unanimously at the
1977th meeting.
