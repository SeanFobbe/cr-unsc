164 (1961). Resolution of 22 July 1961
[S/4882]

The Security Council,

Considering the gravity of the situation prevailing
in Tunisia,

Pending the conclusion of the debate of the item
on its agenda,

1. Calls for an immediate cease-fire and a return of
all armed forces to their original position;

2. Decides to continue the debate.

Adopted at the 962nd meeting
by 10 votes to none. 8

8 One member (France) did not participate in the voting.
