Resolution 299 (1971)
of 30 September 1971

The Security Council,

Having examined the application of Oman 66 for admission to the United Nations,

Recommends to the General Assembly that Oman
be admitted to membership in the United Nations,

Adopted unanimously at the
1587th meeting.

66 Ibid., Supplement for April, May and June 1971, document S/10216.
