Resolution 200 (1965)
of 15 March 1965

The Security Council,

Having examined the application of the Gambia for
admission to the United Nations, 40

Recommends to the General Assembly that the Gambia
be admitted to membership in the United Nations.

Adopted unanimously, at the
1190th meeting.


40 Official Records of the Security Council, Twentieth Year,
Supplement for January, February and March 1965, document
S/6197.
