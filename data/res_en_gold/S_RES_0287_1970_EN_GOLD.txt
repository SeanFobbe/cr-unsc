Resolution 287 (1970)
of 10 October 1970

The Security Council,
Having examined the application of Fiji for admission to the United Nations, 47

Recommends to the General Assembly that Fiji be admitted to membership
in the United Nations.

Adopted unanimously at the
1554th meeting.



47 See Official Records the Security Council, Twenty-fifth Year, Supplement for
October, November and December 1970, document S/9957.
