Resolution 376 (1975)
of 17 October 1975

The Security Council,
Having examined the application of the Comoros 51
for admission to the United Nations,

Recommends to the General Assembly that the Comoros be admitted to membership in the United Nations.

Adopted at the 1848th meet-
ing by 14 votes to none. 52
