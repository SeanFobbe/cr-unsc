Resolution 394 (1976)
of 16 August 1976

The Security Council,

Having examined the application of the Republic of Seychelles 79 for admission to the
United Nations,

Recommends to the General Assembly that the Republic of Seychelles be admitted to
membership in the United Nations.

Adopted unanimously at the
1952nd meeting.

79 Official Records of the Security Council, Thirty-first Year, Supplement for July, August and
September 1976, document S/12164.
