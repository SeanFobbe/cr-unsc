124 (1957). Resolution of 7 March 1957
[S/3801]

The Security Council,

Having considered the application of Ghana for membership in the United Nations, 16

Recommends to the General Assembly that Ghana
be admitted to membership in the United Nations.

Adopted unanimously at the
775th meeting.

16 Official Records of the Security Council, Twelfth Year, Supplement for January, February and March 1957, document S/3797.
