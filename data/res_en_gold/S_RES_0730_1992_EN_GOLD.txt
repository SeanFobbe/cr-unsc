Resolution 730 (1992)
of 16 January 1992
The Security Council,

Recalling its resolution 719 (1991) of 6 November 1991,

Recalling also its resolution 729 (1992) of 14 January 1992,

1. Approves the report of the Secretary-General of 14
January 1992; 7

2. Decides, in accordance with the recommendation in
paragraph 7 of the report, to terminate the mandate of the
United Nations Observer Group in Central America with effect
from 17 January 1992.

Adopted unanimously at the 3031st
meeting.
