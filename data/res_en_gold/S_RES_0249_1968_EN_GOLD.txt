Resolution 249 (1968)
of 18 April 1968

The Security Council,

Having examined the application of Mauritius for admission to the United
Nations (S/8466) ,45

Recommends to the General Assembly that Mauritius be admitted to membership in the United Nations.

Adopted unanimously at the 1414th
meeting.



45 See Official Records of the Security Council, Twenty-third Year, Supplement for
January, February and March 1968.
