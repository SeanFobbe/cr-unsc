Resolution 260 (1968)
of 6 November 1968

The Security Council,

Having examined the application of the Republic of Equatorial Guinea for
admission to the United Nations (S/8883), 47

Recommends to the General Assembly that the Republic of Equatorial
Guinea be admitted to membership in the United Nations.

Adopted unanimously at the 1458th
meeting.



47 Ibid., Supplement for October, November and December 1968.
