Resolution 671 (1990)
of 27 September 1990

The Security Council,

Recalling its resolutions 598 (1987) of 20 July 1987, 619
(1988) of 9 August 1988, 631 (1989) of 8 February 1989,
642 (1989) of 29 September 1989 and 651 (1990) of 29
March 1990,

Having considered the report of the Secretary-General
on the United Nations Iran-Iraq Military Observer Group
of 21 September 1990,‚Äù4 and taking note of the observations expressed therein,

1. Decides to extend the mandate of the United Nations
Iran-Iraq Military Observer Group for a further period of
two months, that is, until 30 November 1990, as recommended by the Secretary-General;

2. Requests the Secretary-General to submit, during
November, a report on his further consultations with the
parties about the future of the Military Observer Group,
together with his recommendations on this matter.
