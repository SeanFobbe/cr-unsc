Resolution 257 (1968)
of 11 September 1968

The Security Council,

Having examined the application of Swaziland for admission to the United
Nations (S/8808), 46

Recommends to the General Assembly that Swaziland be admitted to membership in the United Nations.

Adopted unanimously at the 1450th
meeting.


46 Ibid., Supplement for July, August and September 1968.
