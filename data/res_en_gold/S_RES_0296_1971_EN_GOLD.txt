Resolution 296 (1971)
of 18 August 1971

The Security Council,
Having examined the application of Bahrain 63 for
admission to the United Nations,

Welcomes the application of Bahrain and recommends to the General Assembly that Bahrain be admitted to membership in the United Nations.

Adopted unanimously at the
1575th meeting.

63 Ibid., Supplement for July, August and September 1971, document S/10291
