Resolution 652 (1990)
of 17 April 1990

The Security Council,

Having examined the application of the Republic of
Namibia for admission to the United Nations, 120

Recommends to the General Assembly that the Republic
of Namibia be admitted to membership in the United
Nations.

Adopted unanimously at the
2918th meeting

120 Official Records of the Security Council, Forty-fifth Year, Supplement for April, May and June 1990, document S/21241.
