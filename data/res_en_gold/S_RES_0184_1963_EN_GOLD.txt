184 (1963). Resolution of 16 December 1963
[S/5486]

The Security Council,

Having examined the application of Zanzibar for
membership in the United Nations, 17

Recommends to the General Assembly that Zanzibar
be admitted to membership in the United Nations.

Adopted unanimously at the
1084th meeting.


17 Ibid., Supplement for October, November and December 1963, document S/5478.
