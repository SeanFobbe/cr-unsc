86 (1950). Resolution of 26 September 1950

The Security Council
Finds that the Republic of Indonesia is a peace-loving
State which fulfils the conditions laid down in Article 4
of the Charter of the United Nations, and therefore
recommends to the General Assembly that the Republic
of Indonesia be admitted to membership of the United
Nations.

Adopted at the 503rd meeting
by 10 votes to none, with 1
abstention (China).
