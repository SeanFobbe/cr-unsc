Resolution 373 (1975)
of 18 August 1975

The Security Council,

Having examined the application of the Democratic
Republic of Sao Tome and Principe 46 for admission
to the United Nations,

Recommends to the General Assembly that the Democratic Republic of Sao Tome and Principe be admitted to membership in the United Nations.

Adopted unanimously at the
1338th meeting.

46 Ibid., document S/11804.
