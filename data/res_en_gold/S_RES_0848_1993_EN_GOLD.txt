Resolution 848 (1993)
of 8 July 1993

The Security Council

Having examined the application of the Principality of Andorra for
admission to the United Nations, 17
Recommends to the General Assembly that the Principality of
Andorra be admitted to membership in the United Nations.

Adopted without a vote at the 3251st meeting.


17 Official Records of the Security Council, Forty-eighth
Year, Supplement for July. August and September 1993, document
S/26039.
