Resolution 433 (1978)
of 17 August 1978

The Security Council,

Having examined the application of Solomon Islands 75 for admission to the United Nations,

Recommends to the General Assembly that Solomon
Islands be admitted to membership in the United
Nations.

Adopted unanimously at the
2084th meeting.

75 Official Records of the Security Council, Thirty-third Year, Supplement for July, August and September 1978, document S/12801.
