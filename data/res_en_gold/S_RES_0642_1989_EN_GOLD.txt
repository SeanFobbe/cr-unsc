Resolution 642 (1989)
of 29 September 1989

The Security Council,

Recalling its resolutions 598 (1987) of 20 July 1987,
619 (1988) of 9 August 1988 and 631 (1989) of 8 February 1989,

Having considered the report of the Secretary-General on the United Nations Iran-Iraq Military Observer
Group of 22 September 1989, 74 and taking note of the
observations expressed therein,

Decides:

(a) To call once again upon the parties concerned
to implement immediately Security Council resolution
598 (1987);

(b) To extend the mandate of the United Nations
Iran-Iraq Military Observer Group for a further period
of six months, that is, until 31 March 1990;

(c) To request the Secretary-General to submit, at
the end of this period, a report on the developments in
the situation and the measures taken to implement
resolution 598 (1987).

Adopted unanimously at the
2885th meeting.
