172 (1962). Resolution of 26 July 1962
[S/5149]

The Security Council,

Having examined the application of the Republic of
Rwanda for admission to the United Nations, 12

Recommends to the General Assembly that the
Republic of Rwanda be admitted to membership in the
United Nations.

Adopted unanimously at the
1017th meeting.

12 Official Records of the Security Council, Seventeenth Year,
Supplement for July, August and September 1962, documents
S/5137 and Add.1 and 2.
