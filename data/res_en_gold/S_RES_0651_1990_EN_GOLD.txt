Resolution 651 (1990)
of 29 March 1990

The Security Council,

Recalling its resolutions 598 (1987) of 20 July 1987, 619
(1988) of 9 August 1988, 631 (1989) of 8 February 1989
and 642 (1989) of 29 September 1989,

Having considered the report of the Secretary-General
on the United Nations Iran-Iraq Military Observer Group
of 22 March 1990, 72 and taking note of the observations
expressed therein,

Decides:

(a) To call once again upon the parties concerned to
implement immediately its resolution 598 (1987);

(b) To extend the mandate of the United Nations Iran-Iraq Military Observer Group for a further period of six
months, that is, until 30 September 1990;

(c) To request the Secretary-General to submit, at the
end of this period, a report on the developments in the
situation and the measures taken to implement resolution
598 (1987).

Adapted unanimously at the
2916th meeting.


72 Ibid., document S/21200.
