Resolution 304 (1971)
of 8 December 1971

The Security Council,

Having examined the application of the United Arab
Emirates 67 for admission to the United Nations,

Recommends to the General Assembly that the
United Arab Emirates be admitted to membership in
the United Nations.

Adopted unanimously at the
1609th meeting.

67 Ibid., Supplement for October, November and December
1971, document S/10420.
