Resolution 589 (1986)
of 10 October 1986

The Security Council,
Having considered the question of the recommendation for the appointment of the
Secretary-General of the United Nations,
Recommends to the General Assembly that Mr. Javier Perez de Cuellar be appointed Secretary-General of the United Nations for a second term of office from
1 January 1987 te 31 December 1991.

Adopted unanimously at the
2714th (closed) meeting
