197 (1964). Resolution of 30 October 1964
[S/6033]

The Security Council,

Having examined the application of the Republic of
Zambia for admission to the United Nations, 30

Recommends to the General Assembly that the Republic
of Zambia be admitted to membership in the United
Nations.

Adopted unanimously at the
1161st meeting.


30 Ibid., Supplement for October, November and December 1964,
document S/6025.
