Resolution 374 (1975)
of 18 August 1975

The Security Council,

Having examined the application of the People’s Republic of Mozambique 47 for admission to the United
Nations,

Recommends to the General Assembly that the People’s Republic of Mozambique be admitted to membership in the United Nations.

Adopted unanimously at the
1838th meeting.


47 Ibid., document S/11805.
