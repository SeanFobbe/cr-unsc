160 (1960). Resolution of 7 October 1960
[S/4549]

The Security Council,

Having examined the application of the Federation
of Nigeria, 35
Recommends to the General Assembly that the Federation of Nigeria be admitted to membership in the
United Nations.

Adopted unanimously at the
908th meeting.
